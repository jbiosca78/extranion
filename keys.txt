You control a spaceship with cursor keys, you have two fire buttons,
one for normal fire and one for fast fire. Fast fire will consume charge.
You gain charge and points killing enemies.
You starts with two lifes, every 10,000 points you gain an extra life.

The key mapping is as follows:

Keys         opt1   opt2  opt3
-------------------------------
Move up      up     e     q
Move down    down   d     a
Move left    left   s     o
Move right   right  f     p
Normal fire  c      n     space
Fast fire    x      m     alt
